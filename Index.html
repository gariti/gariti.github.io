about me

I have always been interested in technology since I was a little kid. To me a computer was just a toy that can be used for fun. I always fiddled with my settings on the machine to make it my own, which eventually led me to modifying my games and programs to work the way I wanted. As an adult, I pursued my other interests such as travelling around the world and seeing how others live. I have always been interested in cutting edge technologies to make life easier for people. I think we are just at the beginning of this phase in technology. A dream of mine would be to work on these kind of cutting edge projects.
